Methodology

This report synthesises responses across four instruments: a 25-item Big Five (OCEAN) personality inventory, an 18-item Holland (RIASEC) interest survey, a life-priorities calibration, and a financial-reality filter. Scores are normalised to a 0–100 scale.

Interpretation & Validity

Psychometric results describe tendencies, not limits. They are most useful as a structured starting point for conversation with mentors, family and counsellors — not as a verdict. Revisit this assessment as your experience grows.
